03.02.03

Storyboard

Storyboard is usually one of our requirements for hand because it makes whole process of creating a project easier. It is a tool which helps you to communicate with teachers, team, customer, the target audience, to show your ideas and explain your visions. The story board gives a good overview, and a lot of details as well in a production. This storyboard is a manuscript for video which is a part of website we created for project 3.02: A Bicycle Guide for non-Danes.

Link to site

Another group project with topic to make website focused on newcomers in Denmark to have access to a site with information and an instructional video about the biking rules in Copenhagen. The site had to follow the Copenhagen Municipality Design Guide. We chose bright and fresh colours because we focused on young foreign expands as our target group. The video and the site complement each other both in looks and message.
